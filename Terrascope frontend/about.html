About the Platform

What is TerraScope?

TerraScope is a decision-support platform designed to help farmers and agronomists understand crop and soil conditions using satellite data.

Data Sources

  • Sentinel-2 multispectral satellite imagery
  • Field-level location selection
  • Conceptual NIR-based soil analysis (future scope)

Important Note

TerraScope provides advisory insights and does not replace laboratory soil testing or professional agronomic consultation.
